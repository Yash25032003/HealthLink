Why Choose HealthLInk as Your Social Network for Health?

Increase commitment to health and wellness goals

HealthLink is an online community that believes that the biggest investment individuals can make is in their personal health and in the health of the ones they love, thus improving the quality as well as quantity of life. By connecting and collaborating with family and loved ones a more intensive health and wellness environment is created with accountability being maintained for progression towards wellness goals.

Reassure loved ones

HealthLink seeks to bridge the communication gap created by America’s increasingly mobile society. Value is created by tracking members’ wellness data to improve communication, and provide a peace of mind and a means for family and loved ones to actively participate in health and wellness strategies.

Reduce likelihood of health and wellness disaster

HealthLink ’s model focuses on technology, social networking, and other business model innovations to increase communication and commitment to healthy living and reduce preventable health disasters by providing early warning and notification of loved ones when targets are not being met are not being met and further evaluation is possibly needed.
